Home Rules

At Midnight Empire, our mission is to unite the community by creating a safe, enjoyable, and memorable environment for all. To uphold this standard and ensure that everyone can fully enjoy our events, it is essential that all drivers adhere to our rules.

These guidelines are in place not only to protect the wellbeing of all participants, but also to ensure the best possible experience for everyone involved.

RULES

1. Always represent Midnight Empire positively. Actions that harm our reputation may lead to immediate removal without warning.

2. Sponsor benefits are for paid members only and must be used on your own vehicle. Friends and family must join to access these perks—have them contact us.

3. No bullying or anti-social behaviour. We are a family-friendly group built on respect and shared passion.

4. A valid driver’s licence is required to be a member. If you don’t have one, please let us know immediately.

5. Follow cruise etiquette - Follow the lead cars—distinguishable with special Midnight Empire stickers—to maintain safe and respectful cruise behaviour.

6. Drive responsibly at all times. Skids, weaving, hooning, or any reckless behaviour will not be tolerated at meet locations. These actions may result in immediate removal without warning.

Any questions or concerns can be raised to the Midnight Empire Street Crew members.
